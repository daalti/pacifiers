How to sterilize nuk pacifiers? Can you boil nuk pacifiers?

There are several methods you can use to sterilize NUK pacifiers:

2 - Dishwasher: Place the pacifiers in the top rack of your dishwasher and run a normal wash cycle. Make sure to use a detergent that is safe for babies.

3 - Steam sterilization: Some NUK pacifiers come with a microwave steam sterilization bag. Follow the instructions on the bag to sterilize the pacifiers in the microwave.

4 - Cold water and vinegar: Fill a small bowl with cold water and add 1 teaspoon of white vinegar. Soak the pacifiers in the solution for 5-10 minutes, then rinse them thoroughly with cold water and allow them to air dry.
