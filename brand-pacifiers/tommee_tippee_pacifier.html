How to sterilize tommee tippee pacifiers?

Sterilizing your Tommee Tippee pacifiers is super easy! Here are a few options:

- Use a microwave sterilizer: Just pop your pacifiers in the sterilizer and follow the instructions on the package. This should only take a couple of minutes.

- Boil them: Fill up a pot with water and bring it to a boil. Gently place the pacifiers in the water using a slotted spoon or tongs. Boil them for about 5 minutes, then take them out and let them cool on a clean surface.

- Cold water sterilization: Fill a clean container with water and add a sterilizing solution according to the package instructions. Soak the pacifiers in the solution for the recommended amount of time (usually around 10-15 minutes). Rinse them off with cold water before using.

No matter which method you choose, just make sure to follow the instructions and handle the pacifiers carefully. Regular sterilization is a must to keep bacteria at bay and keep your baby's pacifiers clean and safe.
